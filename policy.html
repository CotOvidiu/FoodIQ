Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 14, 2025
Last updated: July 2, 2025

FoodIQ ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal data when you use our mobile application ("the App").

We comply with applicable data protection laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

1. Information We Collect

When you first use the App, we may collect the following personal data:

  • Name
  • Age
  • Weight
  • Desired weight
  • Dietary goals
  • Food-related data (e.g. photos of meals, macro nutrient data)
  • Device identifiers and analytics (via third-party services)

We also collect:

  • Photos taken through the App
  • Food macro details analyzed from the photos
  • Timestamps and user-generated logs (stored locally and in cloud databases)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Generate personalized nutrition and macro data
  • Track your progress toward dietary goals
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Provide core app functionality
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

We process your data based on:

  • Consent – provided during onboarding
  • Legitimate interest – enhancing user experience
  • Contractual necessity – delivering app services
  • Legal obligation – where required by law

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following services which may collect anonymized or device-level data:

  • Google Play Services
  • Firebase Analytics

5. Data Storage & Security

We store your data securely using encryption and standard security protocols. Food photos and macro data are stored locally and/or in cloud servers. No method is 100% secure, but we follow best practices to minimize risk.

6. Your Rights Under GDPR

If you're in the EU or EEA, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request data deletion
  • Restrict or object to processing
  • Export your data (data portability)

To exercise these rights, contact us at: cospintus@gmail.com

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If we learn that data has been collected, it will be deleted immediately.

8. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

The App may use cookies or similar technologies for analytics. You can disable cookies via your device settings, though this may limit functionality.

9. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as needed to provide the app's services or comply with legal obligations. You may request deletion at any time.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Major changes will be communicated in-app or via email. The latest version will always be accessible within the app and our website.
